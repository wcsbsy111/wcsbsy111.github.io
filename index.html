I am currently a senior undergraduate student at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, majoring in Industrial Design, with a minor in Public Administration at Chu Kochen Honors College. I will graduate in June 2026 with a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) degree. I am actively seeking RA, MPhil, or direct-PhD opportunities. If you are interested in my background, please feel free to contact me at wyufeng@zju.edu.cn.

My research interests are interdisciplinary, spanning design, computer science, and social sciences. I focus on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), personal fabrication, and Human-Robot Interaction (HRI). I am particularly interested in developing interactive systems that bridge digital intelligence and physical experiences, with the goal of enabling users to create adaptive, intelligent, and personalized solutions for real-world applications.

I have published papers at top-tier conferences such as UIST and CHI, and I am eager to collaborate on new projects in these and related areas.
